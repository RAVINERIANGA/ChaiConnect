Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChaiConnect? ▼
ChaiConnect is a digital platform that connects tea farmers, factory staff, and agricultural extension officers in Kenya, enabling efficient management of tea farming, production, delivery, and quality tracking.
Is ChaiConnect a mobile app? ▼
Currently, ChaiConnect is web-based and accessible via browsers. However, a USSD version is under development to support users without smartphones.
Who can use ChaiConnect? ▼
ChaiConnect can be used by registered tea farmers, factory staff, and extension officers. Each user has a personalized dashboard tailored to their role.
How do I register as a farmer? ▼
Click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. Your details will be reviewed and approved by the system administrator.
Can I update my delivery information? ▼
Yes. Factory staff can view and update delivery records, and all delivery history is tracked within the system for transparency.
How secure is my data on ChaiConnect? ▼
Data security is a top priority. ChaiConnect uses secure authentication, role-based access, and encrypted storage to protect your data.
How can I give feedback or report issues? ▼
Use the “Contact Us” page or speak to your nearest extension officer. We’re committed to improving your experience with ChaiConnect.
